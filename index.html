Timed Ruling Mode

When enabled, you can link blocks between the plaintiff and defense sides. During a paused timer, you'll have the option to deduct the paused time from either the current side's block or the linked block on the opposing side, simulating the effect of sustained or overruled objections.

You may not want this enabled: check your competition rules. Some simply discard paused time, while others deduct based on the ruling.
